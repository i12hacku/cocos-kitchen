About Us...

Welcome to Cocos Kitchen, where global flavors converge to create an unparalleled dining experience. Located in the heart of Colombo 07, our restaurant celebrates culinary diversity through a fusion of international traditions. Our vibrant dining space invites you to embark on a gastronomic journey, savoring dishes that blend ingredients and techniques from around the world.

From the spicy warmth of Thai curries to the comforting embrace of Italian pasta and the umami richness of Japanese sushi, our chefs expertly craft each dish to showcase the best of every culinary heritage. Our diverse team of passionate chefs brings a wealth of knowledge and authenticity to every plate.

Cocos Kitchen embodies a spirit of inclusivity and hospitality, making it the perfect place for friends and families to come together and create lasting memories. Join us and indulge in a celebration of global flavors, discovering the joy of diversity on every plate. Let your palate guide you through a culinary adventure unlike any other.

Our Vision

Dragon Heritage The Dragon story dates back to 1942, when a family of Chinese immigrants made their way to our island ‘Ceylon’ after days at sea. The new island with the spice-strong cuisine welcomed them with open arms, but these settlers who craved their comfort food from home, found the island flavours interesting, yet lacking in the tastes and textures their hearts craved for.
Driven by their entrepreneurial spirit, these settlers found the perfect marriage between the local island cuisine and their authentic palate, cooking up a storm that was soon in demand by locals and other settlers alike.

Our Mission

While the Battle of Ceylon raged on, a battle in the kitchen ensued in a humble space in the old Commercial Building down Galle Road; the outcome of which is known as Chinese Dragon Café today.
The Café grew and stood salient through the years whilst history unfolded in Sri Lanka. Through the battles and independence, through wars and pandemics, Chinese Dragon Café has been a staple in Sri Lankan food culture; and is today the oldest and most respected restaurant chain in the country.

OUR SERVICES

Dine-In

Traditional table service where customers sit at a table, order from a waiter or waitress, and are served their food at the table.

Take Away

Customers order food to go, either by phone, online, or in person, and pick it up from the restaurant to eat elsewhere.

Delivery

The restaurant delivers food directly to the customer's location, which can be ordered via phone, app, or online.
